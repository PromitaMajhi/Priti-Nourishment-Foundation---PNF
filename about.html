Our Origin

Born from a
Bag of Hope.

The Priti Nourishment Foundation began with a simple but powerful idea: what if we could help families eat better without needing to read? In 2024, a group of passionate young people in Bagerhat launched the Hanging Nutrition Bag Project.

Mission

Targeted nutrition education for vulnerable communities.

Vision

A Bangladesh where every mother has the foundation to thrive.
