Our Core Programs

Strategic pillars for community nutrition and health.

🌿
Flagship

Hanging Nutrition Bag Project

Eco-friendly bags with illustrated food menus designed for even illiterate households to follow healthy meal plans independently.

👩
Health

Girl Health Awareness Sessions

Educational camps focused on adolescent nutrition, menstrual hygiene, and healthy habits in rural Bagerhat.

🥗
Training

Family Nutrition Training

Hands-on workshops for caregivers on affordable healthy cooking using local ingredients.

🌟
Leadership

Youth Nutrition Advocates

Empowering young people to lead health campaigns and speak for their community's nutritional needs.

🍎
Campaign

Healthy Food Campaign

Widespread awareness initiatives promoting local superfoods and low-cost nutritional choices.

📊
Research

Community Research

Scientific data collection within families to understand the root causes of malnutrition in Bagerhat.
